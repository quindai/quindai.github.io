About

Back in 2018, I decided to shift my focus from scientific research to industry software development and data engineering and tumbled head first into SOLID principles. Fast-forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of building software for a health company, a start-up, and for clients of a huge corporation.

My main focus these days is building products and leading projects for our clients at LaCCAN. I most enjoy building software in the sweet spot where complex algorithms and engineering meet — things that look good but are also built well under the hood.

When I’m not at the computer, I’m usually working out at the gym, reading, hanging out with my wife, or cycling on the beach avenue.
